Senshi is a Belgium based brand from Ghent founded in 2020.


In this brand I (Isaac Van de Velde) combined two of my favourite topics namely the Japanese (street)cult and design. And at some point I decided to share that passion by designing and selling products, Senshi was born.


The intention is to offer nice, fair and quality garments with Japanese influences.
